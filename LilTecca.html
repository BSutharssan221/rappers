Lil Tecca

Lil Tecca(Real Name: Tyler-Justin Anthony Sharpe was born on 26 August 2002 in Queens, New York). His first track was Tectri in 2017 featuring Lil Gummybear but his first big track was "Ransom" which debut at Number #4 on the billboard 100 and has 1 billion streams on Spotify

Lil Tecca's early life

Lil Tecca relocated to Cedarhust(a place in Long Island, New York) and he was inspired by artists such as Coldplay, Eminem, Chief Keef etc. He had aspirations of playing in the NBA before transitioning into Music at the age of 12

Lil Tecca's new and unreleased album

Lil Tecca is releasing his new album "Dopamine". However on Google, X etc there have been no plans to release Dopamine but it is expected to be released in 2025. His album will include his latest single Dark Thoughts which is gaining popularity fast like his hit track "Ransom" in 2018 and got a remix with the late Juice WRLD.
